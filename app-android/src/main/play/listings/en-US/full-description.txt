This app adds Halifax Transit (Metro Transit, MetroLink, MetroX) buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the schedule as well as real time major disruptions and news from @hfxtransit on Twitter.

Halifax Transit buses serve Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford and Sackville in Nova Scotia, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://bit.ly/MonTransitPlay

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Halifax Transit and the Halifax Regional Municipality.
https://www.halifax.ca/home/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-halifax-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Halifax Transit and the Halifax Regional Municipality.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time major disruptions and to read news from Twitter
